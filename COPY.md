Clutch Financial provides coaching and education only. It does not provide investment, tax, legal, insurance, or accounting advice.

## About Humair Copy

Humair started Clutch Financial because he knows how much easier life could have been if someone had sat him down earlier and helped him understand the financial basics.

## FAQ Copy

### What is financial coaching?

Financial coaching is practical education, structure, and accountability for the financial decisions you are responsible for making. It can include budgeting, cash flow, 401(k), Roth IRA, HSA, 529, equity compensation decision support, major purchase planning, and follow-through.

### Is this the same as financial planning?

No. Clutch Financial provides coaching and education only. It does not provide investment, tax, legal, insurance, or accounting advice. When a question requires a licensed professional, the coaching process can help you identify that need.

### Who is coaching best for?

Coaching is designed for early-career professionals, professionals, couples, and growing families who are doing fine on paper but still feel uncertain, behind, or disorganized financially.

### Do you publish package pricing?

Not yet. Clutch Financial starts with an introductory consultation so the conversation can focus on fit, priorities, and the kind of support that would actually help.

### Will I be judged for past money decisions?

No. The work is calm, practical, and judgment-free. The goal is not shame or perfection. The goal is progress you can sustain.
