# Project Brief

## Project Name

Clutch Financial

## Summary

Clutch Financial is a financial coaching and education brand for early-career professionals, professionals, and growing families who want stronger financial habits, better decisions, and more follow-through.

The website should feel premium, modern, calm, and practical. It should make visitors feel like they can sit down with a trusted thinking partner and make sense of the financial decisions in front of them.

## Primary Goal

Create a professional website that helps qualified visitors schedule an introductory consultation.

The site should:

- Establish trust quickly.
- Explain who Clutch Financial helps.
- Make clear that the work is coaching and education, not advisory services.
- Communicate the core philosophy: structure, accountability, and follow-through.
- Avoid hype, guru language, or sales pressure.

## Target Audience

Primary audience:

- Age range roughly 25 to 45.
- Early-career professionals with first real income.
- Professionals getting married or recently married.
- Couples having or planning for kids.
- Growing families making bigger financial decisions.
- Tech and professional workers with equity compensation.
- People who are doing fine but still feel uncertain, behind, or disorganized financially.

## Core Positioning

Clutch Financial helps professionals and growing families build strong financial habits, make confident financial decisions, and follow through on the actions that create long-term wealth.

Clutch Financial is not positioned as a broad retirement planning firm. Retirement savings can be mentioned as part of long-term planning, but the brand should avoid retirement income planning, Social Security, estate planning, tax planning, insurance advice, or investment advisory services.

## Core Philosophy

Most people do not need more random financial information. They need structure, accountability, and help following through.

Prominent line:

You probably already know some of what you should be doing. My job is to help you actually do it.

## Website Structure

Core pages:

- Home
- About
- Services
- FAQ
- Contact

Homepage focus:

- Who Clutch helps.
- What problem it solves.
- Why it is trustworthy.
- Main CTA.

## CTA

Primary CTA:

Schedule an Introductory Consultation

Supporting copy:

A complimentary 30-minute conversation to understand your goals, answer your questions, and determine whether Clutch Financial is the right fit.

## Required Disclaimer

Clutch Financial provides financial coaching and education only. It does not provide investment, tax, legal, insurance, or accounting advice.
