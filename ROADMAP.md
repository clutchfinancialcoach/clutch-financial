# Roadmap

## Current Phase

Phase 1: Professional Website

The current priority is a polished public website that clearly explains who Clutch Financial helps, what problem it solves, and how to schedule an introductory consultation.

## Phase 1: Professional Website

Goals:

- Present Clutch Financial as a warm, practical, trustworthy coaching brand.
- Focus positioning on professionals and growing families, not broad retirement planning.
- Explain the core philosophy: structure, accountability, and follow-through.
- Use the official logo and logo-aligned blue/green visual system.
- Keep the homepage concise.
- Move deeper founder content to About.
- Move detailed service explanations to Services.
- Move detailed questions to FAQ.
- Use the approved CTA: Schedule an Introductory Consultation.

Core pages:

- Home
- About
- Services
- FAQ
- Contact

Status: In progress

Quality practices:

- Keep pages focused and readable.
